About me

Based in Winnipeg Manitoba, I am a Software Developer with a passion for building practical, engaging and efficient solutions. With a background spanning multiple industries, I bring a unique perspective to development, combining technical know-how with adaptability, creativity and a sharp eye for detail.

I enjoy building tools and projects that add value; from a return on investment calculator to generate leads, to a banner builder that streamlines client satisfaction. I’ve developed, refactored and debugged live production sites under pressure, and delivered smooth, reliable solutions on tight deadlines. My toolkit spans the basic three (HTML, CSS and JavaScript) along with C#, MySQL, .NET, React and more. I am open to learning anything and everything. At my core, I thrive on breaking down challenges, streamlining workflows, and creating solutions that are both powerful, and user-friendly.

My journey into the development sphere wasn’t by any means linear, and I see that as one of my strengths. From kitchen to customer support, I’ve learnt discipline, teamwork and the importance of precision. Cooking has taught me that the smallest of details frequently define what the final result may be, and that lesson translates directly into how I approach writing clear, clean and dependable code. Those experiences in life also taught me how to stay calm under pressure, communicate clearly, and deliver consistent results when it matters the most.

Outside of coding, I like playing board games, where planning, adaptability and problem-solving determine success. I also enjoy culinary challenges, like having a handful of pre-determined ingredients and crafting something both structured and creative, a process that mirrors how I approach development projects. Whether it’s designing features, debugging code, or experimenting with new technology, I see each challenge as a puzzle waiting to be solved.

Looking ahead, I am open to opportunities throughout a full-stack web development, software engineering, AI-driven applications, game development or really, anything. What excites me most is contributing to projects that push me to grow, demand creative solutions, and ultimately make a meaningful impact for users.
